ITIS 3135 Project Info

Project Overview

The purpose of this website is to promote but also be a homepage for the band to use. So if someone who heard of them or saw their name on a poster. Could look them up and find this website for a quick introduction to them and their music.
The intended users for this site is anyone who is a fan, wants to be a fan, or anyone who wants to work with the band. For example if someone wanted them to headline their show or to hire them. Could come here and check out their songs and message them using the contact page.
The website will have a page for their videos from their recent album, a gallery of pictures from shows/band, an introduction, and a contact page.

Page Design

Page: What

This page is a small introduction to the band and the website. It is the page the website opens up to. There is a single image and no interactable elements besides the nav bar.

Page: Gallery

This page will have a functioning gallery of photos of the band and photos from their shows. It will have interactable gallery buttons to move through photos and a smaller section displaying all images. Which can be clicked on to display large.

Page: Videos

This page will have a embeded youtube video of one of their songs but also a link to their youtube page. With a button that unhides some backstage videos.

Page: Who We Are

This page will have a full introduction of who the band is and its members with a group photo at the top. Will also have Social Media buttions for the band, but these will also be in the Nav bar.

Page: Contact Us

This will be a form with 3 inputs being name, email, and message. That will send them an email with their message included in it.

Dynamic functionality on the website

This page will use javascript for making a working gallery for the gallery page. But also have a function contact form for messaging the band. There will also be playable videos from their recent album on the video page similar to this page https://arcticmonkeys.com/.
