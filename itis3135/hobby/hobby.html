How I got into hockey

How it started

As a kid I always loved watching movies and especially films that weren't from my families country of Mexico. Not because I didn't like the films but they were so much different than the ones being shown. So when I found a very enjoyable film I usually gained an obsession of the thing being depicted. Which is why I'm big into hockey, cars, and music. But we aren't here to talk about the other two. In my home a film that always came on the television was the Mighty Ducks films. Now for me as being part of the first generation born here in the US instead of Mexico. Found this story of a rag tag team of kids who went on to win it all, amazing.
