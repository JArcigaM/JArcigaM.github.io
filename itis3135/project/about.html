Introduction

Weymouth is a 5 piece indie punk band from central NC. We try to encapsulate the popular alternative sounds of late 2000s to early 2010s while refreshing it with a modern twist. Weymouth focuses on a high energy and engaging performance. We wish to provide an audience with an experience they can cherish and hopefully inspire others to start bands themselves.
